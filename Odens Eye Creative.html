About Me

I am an Army Combat Veteran who proudly served for 5 years. I held the rank of Specialist and deployed to both Iraq and Afghanistan before completing my service in 2012.

My experiences in combat and service to my country continue to shape the way I approach life, creativity, and legacy. Every design I create is built with the same discipline, authenticity, and respect that guided me in uniform.
